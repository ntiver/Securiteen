Helping you to find peace

At Securiteen, we wanted to keep things as easy as possible for our customers, after all, life with children is already stressful enough!

That's why we came up with a simple hourly rate for any work which is carried out by our staff. Whether that is implementing part or all of an action plan following one of our consultations, or simply helping you to configure a new device with parental controls.  Our rate is just $75 per hour.

Any additional hardware that you require will be billed at cost plus a 15% margin, and any necessary software or subscription services from third parties will be payable by you directly with the vendor.

This ensures transparency and clarity in all our dealings.
